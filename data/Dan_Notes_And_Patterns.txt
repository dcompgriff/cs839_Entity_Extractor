Dan Notes and Patterns:
*"the" comes before
*"from" comes before 
*"called" comes before
*"they" comes after
*"reported", "notes", "reviewed" and similar words comes after (But not "said", as these are people)
*"by" comes before (This may cause some issues though, as it could be cause names of people to occur)
*"named", "entity"
*"group", "organization", "society", "founded", "formed" nearby
*".org", ".com", ".co", "company", "corporation", "website", "nonprofit" before or after.
*Capital letters
